A Collaboration Rooted in Purpose

Deliver Your Style is proud to support the mission of House of Good Deeds — a nonprofit dedicated to helping those in need with compassion, dignity, and community care.

Styling with Impact

Deliver Your Style is a professional, for-profit styling business. Our services — including private styling, virtual consultations, curated boxes, and more — are offered at standard rates with a high-touch, personalized approach.

Through a special collaboration with House of Good Deeds, a nonprofit organization led by our founder’s partner, a **portion of proceeds from every purchase directly supports their free community initiatives**.

*Please note: Deliver Your Style is not a nonprofit, and styling services are not provided for free.*

How Your Style Makes a Difference

When you book a styling service or make a purchase, you're not just investing in your wardrobe — you're helping provide essentials like clothing, food, and hygiene items to individuals and families in need.

50% Showroom Proceeds

Half of the revenue from showroom purchases is donated to House of Good Deeds.

Complimentary Styling with Purpose

Clients spending $300+ receive a free styling session, while contributing to charity.

Kindness Multiplied

Together, we empower self-confidence, sustainability, and social good through fashion.

“Style can be a tool for self-expression — and a bridge to help others rise. Through this collaboration, we bring beauty and meaning together.”
